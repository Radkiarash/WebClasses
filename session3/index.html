Govaresh

Govaresh dar jandaran baste be noe anha az tanavvo va goonagooni ziadi bartkhordar ast va damaneye in goonagooni az tak celluli ha gerefte ta pestandaran va sayere jandarane por celluli gostaresh miyabad.

Trichodinou

Yek jandare tak celluli ke govareshe boroon celluli va sepas daroun cellu darad. in jandar ba komak mozhak haye khod harkat mikonad va niz ba komak tazhake khod shekar ra be samte hofreye dahani khod mikeshad.

Nokate marboot be Trickodinaou

  1. yek tak celluli ast
  2. az jomle jandarane mozhak dar ast
  3. jozve aghaziyan mibashad

Ensan

Darbareye govaresh dar ensan nokate zir haeze ahamiyyat mibashad

  • tafiqi az govareshe daroun va boron celluli dara mibashad
  • daraye yek loole govareshe yek tarafe ast
